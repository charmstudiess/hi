Charm Studies

A downloadable game for Windows, macOS, and Linux

Download NowName your own price

CHARM STUDIES is a lo-fi picross game about young witches studying magic. you play as cassia, an airheaded girl who's currently failing charms class... luckily, senna has agreed to tutor her! the only problem is she's not very nice... can these two little witches learn from each other through the magic of puzzles?

the game includes a small tutorial for those who are new to picross, but you won't fail if you make mistakes either. you can still see all of the story regardless of your picross skills.

i hope that all players will be able to relax and enjoy the pleasant vibes!

FEATURES

  • 15 puzzles, 3 difficulty levels
  • 8k wordcount
  • a cute and chill lo-fi soundtrack

made for NaNoRenO 2023!

listen to the soundtrack on youtube, or buy it on bandcamp!
